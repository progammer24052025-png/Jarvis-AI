VALORANT COMPLETE GAMEPLAN & GUIDE (2026)
I. GENERAL TIPS & TRICKS (BEGINNER TO PRO)
1. Fundamentals (Beginners)
Stop before you shoot: You are inaccurate while moving. Use counter-strafing (tapping the opposite movement key to stop instantly).
Crosshair Placement: Keep your crosshair at head level always. Don't look at the floor. Anticipate where enemy heads will be.
Economy Management: Buy with your team. If they are saving, you save. Generally, buy in rounds where you have 3900+ credits (Rifle + Armor).
Don't panic spray: Burst fire (2-3 bullets) at long range. Spray only at close range.
Use the mini-map: It provides massive information about enemy positions and utility usage.

2. Tactical Advancement (Intermediate/Pros)
Trading Kills: Never take a fight alone. If a teammate dies, you should be in a position to immediately kill the enemy who shot them.
Map Control & Defaulting: Do not rush every round. Spread out, take space, and bait out enemy utility before committing to a site.
Utility Efficiency: Use utility to set up kills, not just for damage. Pair flashes with dualist entries or stuns with mollys.
Post-Plant Management: Use your utility to stall time rather than for kills. Mollies on the spike delay defusers.
Audio Discipline: Shift-walk to avoid giving away your position, unless you are deliberately trying to make noise to rotate the enemy team.

II. ROLE-BASED GAMEPLAN
A. Duelists (Jett, Phoenix, Reyna, Raze, Yoru, Neon, Iso) 
Goal: Create space, enter sites, get entry frags.
Attack: Be the first in. Use abilities to flash or smoke for yourself. Your goal is to distract defenders and secure site entry.
Defense: Play aggressively to take map control or hold high-risk angles to get an early pick, then dash/teleport back to safety.

III. COMPLETE GAMEPLAN (SIDE-SPECIFIC) 
1. Attacking Side Strategy
Pistol Round (R1): Stick together, rush one site quickly, and aim for trades.
Default (R2-R4): Take control of key map areas (e.g., Mid on Ascent) to force defenders to waste utility and reveal their positions.
Lurking: Send one player (usually a Sentinel or Controller) to alternate routes to kill rotating defenders or take space.
Site Execute: 1. Smoke choke points -> 2. Initiate (Flash/Stun) -> 3. Duelist enters -> 4. Plant.
Post-Plant: Get behind cover. Play for time. Use mollies and damage utility on the spike.

2. Defending Side Strategy
Early Info: Use a Recon tool (Sova, Fade) or an aggressive player (Jett/Reyna) to find out where the enemy is early.
Holding Angles: Use varied, unconventional angles to keep attackers guessing.
Retake Setup: If a site is lost, do not die alone. Wait for teammates and retake together with full utility usage (smokes/flashes).
Utility Dump: If the enemy rushes, use all your damage abilities (Grenades, Mollies) to stop them before they enter.
